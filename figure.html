Historical Figure: Sergey Brin

Sergery brin, a 47 year old born in Moscow, Russia in 1973, is an American Computer Science and Entrepreneur who immigrated to America at the age of six. He founded Alphabet Inc and created Google with Larry Page. He grew up in a household with a family emphasis on intelligence and education. Influenced by his parents, he was a pretty successful student who was admitted to the University of Marylands where he received a degree in Mathematics and Computer Science. From there, he went to Stanford University where he met Larry Page and started his prosperous career and became financially successful.
